About Me

My Idea

I choose the idea of wand making for this project because it is simple to create,
fun to do and it's Halloween themed. (Considering Halloween is right around the corner)
I came along this idea last year in the University Center. It was Harry Potter week
and there were all kinds of fun booths you could go to. I found the wand making one and
decided I wanted to make my very own.

Other HP Crafts

While browsing the Wizard Web, I found more Harry Potter crafting ideas.
I found DIY ideas for crafting felt house ties for costumes or decorations.
I also found creating your own quil to write with along with eatable mini
broom sticks.
