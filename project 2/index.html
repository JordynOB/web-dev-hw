How To Make a Harry Potter Wand

With these easy steps, you will be able to create your very own realistic Harry Potter Wand! Here are the things you will need.

  • 8 inch piece of wood
  • glue gun
  • tempra or acrlyic paint
  • finishing spray

Lets begin!

    1. First you will need an 8 inch piece of wood about the width of a pencil.
      -Because wands vary in size, whatever length or width is up to you.

          2. Next you will need a glue gun.
            -To create the texture on the wand
            the glue gun will act as a tool to create any
            design you want.

            3. Once you have created your design and let
            it dry, you are ready for paint.
                -Paint it any color you like! Once dry spray with setting spray to give a glossy look.
